How to Use This XRP Dashboard – Your Edge Explained

Global Trade Flow (Glowing Cities)

Every real trade on Binance triggers a city to light up:

  • Green flash → someone just BOUGHT XRP
  • Red flash → someone just SOLD XRP
  • More flashes = hotter volume right now

Live Order Book (Aggregated)

Shows the real buy/sell walls across Binance + OKX + Bybit + Kraken

  • Green Total much bigger → strong support → price likely rises
  • Red Total much bigger → heavy resistance → price likely drops
  • Look for sudden huge Size rows → whales defending or capping price

ETF Inflow Alerts (Top Banner)

Whenever an ETF buys $10M+ in one day → big green banner + all cities flash

  • Each alert removes that XRP from circulation forever
  • More alerts = faster supply shock = higher price pressure
  • Real data (or realistic simulation until full API is public)

Supply Shock Tracker (Bottom)

The most important box on the page

  • Total ETF XRP Locked → XRP permanently removed from exchanges
  • Effective Circulating Supply → real XRP left to trade (watch it fall below 59B)
  • OTC Desks 80% Dry → next big buys must hit exchanges → price moons
  • When locked hits 1–2% → historic squeeze begins

Quick Trading Rules Used by the Pros on This Page:

  1. 1. Green city flashes + growing green Total in order book → buy the dip
  2. 2. Inflow alert appears → hold or add (supply just got tighter)
  3. 3. Red Total suddenly huge → take profits or wait
  4. 4. Locked XRP > 500M and OTC warning red → price about to explode
